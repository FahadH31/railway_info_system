About Us


Welcome to Express Railway Association, where we're passionate about connecting people through the wonder of railways and trains. At ERA, our primary goal is to simplify your travelling experience by providing an intuitive and compact user interface to access any necessary information. All the information you want or need is just a click away!

Our platform isn't just a resource for timetables and routes; it's also a source of educational train information. Whether you're a seasoned professional or have no knowledge of the railway industry, we offer fun facts and historical information to enhance your experience.
